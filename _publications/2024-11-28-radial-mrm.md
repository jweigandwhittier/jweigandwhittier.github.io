excerpt: 'The proposed method consists of segmented saturation modules with radial FLASH readouts and golden angle progression. The segmented saturation blocks drive the system to steady-state, and because center k-space is sampled repeatedly, steady-state saturation dominates contrast during gridding and reconstruction. Ten complete Z-spectra were acquired in healthy mice using both ECG and respiratory-gated and ungated methods. Z-spectra were also acquired at multiple saturation B1 values to optimize for amide and Cr contrasts.'
